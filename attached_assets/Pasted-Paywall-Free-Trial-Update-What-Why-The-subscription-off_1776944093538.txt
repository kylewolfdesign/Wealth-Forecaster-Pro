Paywall Free Trial Update
What & Why
The subscription offering changed: the monthly plan is gone, and the annual plan now includes a 3-day free trial. Update the paywall to reflect a single annual-with-trial offer, with the free trial as the main CTA and Apple-required disclosures in supporting text.

Done looks like
Paywall no longer shows a monthly plan or a plan selector — only the annual plan is presented.
Primary CTA reads as a free trial action (e.g. "Start 3-Day Free Trial").
Directly under the CTA, supporting text discloses: trial length, that it auto-converts to a paid annual subscription after the trial, the per-month equivalent price, the full annual price, and that it is billed annually (e.g. "3 days free, then $X.XX/month ($XX.XX/year, billed annually)").
All prices and the currency shown anywhere on the paywall (CTA subtext, summary block, etc.) are pulled dynamically from the RevenueCat annual package at runtime — no hardcoded amounts, currency symbols, or fallback strings. The per-month equivalent is computed from the annual package's price / 12 and formatted using the package's currency code (via priceString / price + currencyCode from the RevenueCat package), so it automatically reflects the user's locale and any price changes made in the RevenueCat dashboard.
Existing Apple-required legal copy remains visible: auto-renewal terms, cancellation instructions, Restore Purchases, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Use (EULA) links.
Visual style (colors, spacing, typography, card treatment, badges) matches the current paywall — no redesign.
If the annual package fails to load, the existing error / retry / SDK-unavailable states still work.
Purchase, registration, login, and restore flows continue to work end-to-end with the annual package.
Out of scope
Any change to RevenueCat dashboard configuration, product IDs, or trial setup on the store side (assumed already done).
Redesigning the paywall, account form, or legal copy beyond what the new single-plan layout requires.
Changes to non-paywall surfaces (settings, onboarding copy, etc.).
Steps
Remove monthly-plan state, selection, and the plan selector UI; treat annual as the only package and drive purchase directly off it.
Replace the two plan cards with a single annual summary block (reusing existing card styles) showing the trial, per-month equivalent (computed from the annual package price / 12 and formatted with the package's currency code), full annual price (from the package's localized price string), and "billed annually" line. No hardcoded amounts or currency symbols.
Update the primary CTA label and loading state to reflect starting the free trial, and update the disabled-state logic to depend only on the annual package being loaded.
Add the Apple-required trial disclosure line directly beneath the CTA, and keep the existing auto-renew legal paragraph and Privacy/Terms links intact.
Verify the SDK-unavailable, offerings-loading, offerings-error/retry, register-then-purchase, login-then-purchase, and restore flows still behave correctly with the simplified single-plan model.
